## 1. Customer Segments

### Primary Segment (60% of users)
**Small Business Owners**
- Revenue: $300k-$1M/year
- Employees: 5-25
- Examples: Local fitness studios, boutique e-commerce stores, small SaaS startups
- Pain: No time for marketing, can't afford agency ($2k-5k/month)
- ARPU: $49-99/month

### Secondary Segment (30% of users)
**Marketing Managers at SMBs**
- Company revenue: $1M-10M/year
- Company employees: 20-200
- Examples: SaaS companies, professional services, B2B tech
- Pain: Tool sprawl (Canva + Jasper + translation), manual multilingual campaigns
- ARPU: $149-299/month

### Tertiary Segment (10% of users)
**Digital Agencies**
- Clients: 10-50 small businesses
- Team: 5-20 employees
- Examples: Social media agencies, marketing consultancies
- Pain: Manual work per client, no white-label solution
- ARPU: $499-999/month

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks
- **Risk**: AI content generation becomes commoditized
- **Mitigation**: Focus on B2B workflows, multilingual specialization, industry templates

### Technology Risks
- **Risk**: OpenAI API costs increase
- **Mitigation**: Support multiple LLM providers (Anthropic, local models), smart caching

### Competition Risks
- **Risk**: Canva or Jasper adds multilingual campaigns
- **Mitigation**: Move faster, deeper B2B integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce), niche specialization

### Execution Risks
- **Risk**: Can't acquire users at <$100 CAC
- **Mitigation**: Content marketing, referral program, freemium model for viral growth
